Installation Complete

KymFlow is now installed. If this was a first-time setup, package downloads may have taken a few minutes—subsequent installs are usually faster.

Next steps
  1. In Finder, open Documents → KymFlow (or choose Go → Home, then Documents → KymFlow).
  2. Double-click KymFlow Jupyter.app (recommended). JupyterLab will open in your default web browser without a Terminal window. If macOS says the app can’t be opened, right-click the app → Open once to confirm (the app is generated locally and is not separately code-signed).
  3. Alternative: double-click Open KymFlow.command (Terminal opens briefly; same JupyterLab).

Your notebook workspace is ~/Documents/KymFlow. Example notebooks and data are under that folder.
